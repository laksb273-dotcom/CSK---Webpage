About CSK

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional cricket team based in Chennai,Tamil Nadu. It competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and it known for its consistency and powerful leadership under M.S.Dhoni.

Captain:M.S.Dhoni
Coach:Stephen Fleming
Home Ground:M.A.Chidambaram Stadium,Chennai
Owner:India Cements

Key Players

  • M.S. Dhoni (Captain & Wicket Keeper)
  • Ruturaj Gaikwad
  • Ravindra Jadeja
  • Shivam Dube
  • Deepak Chahar
  • Matheesa Pathirana
